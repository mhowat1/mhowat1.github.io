About

I am a Master of Public Administration student at the London School of Economics, specialising in Data Science for Public Policy with a focus on environmental economics and food systems.

My research interests centre on agrifood systems transformation for planetary health, and on how data, econometrics, and machine learning can support evidence- based net-zero policy design.

My work combines data visualisation, causal inference, and applied machine learning to address complex policy and systems-level challenges.

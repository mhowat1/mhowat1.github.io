About Me

I'm a Master of Public Administration student at the London School of Economics, specializing in Data Science for Public Policy with a focus on environmental economics and food systems.

I am most interested in agrifood systems transformations to support planetary health, and how data and machine learning can play a pivotal role in influencing governments and individuals to pursue net-zero policies. My work combines data visualization, econometric analysis, and machine learning techniques to address these complicated task of systems transformation.
